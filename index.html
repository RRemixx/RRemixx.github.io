Publications

  • Neural Conformal Control for Time Series Forecasting
    Ruipu Li, Alexander Rodríguez
    Proceedings of the 39th AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI 2025), 2025.

    A neural-network-based conformal framework for non-stationary time series that treats the model as a controller to achieve target coverage with asymptotic coverage guarantees.

  • Adaptive Conformal Prediction Intervals Over Trajectory Ensembles
    Ruipu Li, Diego Menacho, Alexander Rodríguez
    arXiv preprint, 2025. (In progress)

    A unified conformal approach that transforms sampled trajectory ensembles into calibrated prediction intervals, with an online update step and a trajectory-level optimization step.
